Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data private and secure?

Yes. All database connections are secured with strict Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Only you have permission to view your logged records.

How does the AI Meal Scanner work?

It uploads your plate photo to a local machine learning segmentation server, identifies ingredients, and queries food databases for nutrition values.

Can I customize my training splits?

Absolutely. You can choose resting templates, program exercises, swap movements, and track streaks over time.
